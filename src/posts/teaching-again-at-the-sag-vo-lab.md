The SAG peeps brought it this weekend! I'm honored to have returned to teach voiceover promo at the beautiful Don LaFontaine VO Lab in LA. Everyone stepped up and put their chops on the line. Promo isn't just announcing, it's investing every word about every show with love. Even just a title and a tag is a story in the right hands. Thanks to [Aric Shuford](https://www.linkedin.com/in/aric-shuford-29b7488/), Nat'l Director of Programs, and [Eric Edward Hurst](https://www.linkedin.com/in/eric-edward-hurst-aa379326b/), engineer extraordinaire and accomplished VO, and to the [SAG-AFTRA Foundation](https://www.linkedin.com/company/sag-aftra-foundation/). And to every mentor and teacher of mine, including the immense talents we showcased to demonstrate true excellence. If you haven't checked out all the terrific classes and educational material at SAG, take a gander.
