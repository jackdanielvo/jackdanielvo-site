When it all comes together, it's a beautiful thing. I just cut a spot for the NHL Stanley Cup playoffs on TNT, working with insanely good creatives from Warner Bros Discovery (who I worked with last year on Stanley Cup spots). These guys love what they do and have broad creative control. When they told me they had a two-minute playoff promo for me to voice, I was jazzed for what I knew would be a great session. And it was. Check out the timing and precision of the cuts and the perfect use of music. It doesn't get much better.
